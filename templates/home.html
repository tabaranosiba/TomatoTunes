Welcome to Tomato Tunes!

TomatoTunes is a study playlist creator that uses audio and songs that complement short study sessions. It uses the Pomodoro study technique developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

TomatoTunes allows you to create 20-25 minute playlists, save your 'tomato' timers, and then play them for any study session.
